Mavora Child Safety Standards

Last updated: May 22, 2026

Mavora is an anonymous realtime chat app for adults. Mavora is intended for users who are 18 years of age or older and is not directed to children.

Zero Tolerance

Mavora prohibits child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE), child sexual abuse material (CSAM), grooming, sexualization of minors, solicitation involving minors, and any attempt to share, request, promote, or facilitate this content or behavior.

In-App Reporting

Mavora provides an in-app report action in chat channels. Reports are recorded with moderation metadata so the developer can review abuse or child safety concerns. Users can also contact support directly at hello.jadeai@gmail.com.

Review And Enforcement

Reported content or behavior may result in action including channel review, account restriction, removal of abusive data where technically possible, and preservation of relevant metadata for safety review.

Legal Reporting

When Mavora becomes aware of apparent CSAM or conduct involving child sexual exploitation, we will take appropriate action consistent with applicable law, including reporting to relevant regional or national authorities where required.

Data Handling

Messages are retained for a short period as described in the Mavora Privacy Policy. Moderation reports may retain relevant chat metadata longer where needed to review safety concerns and comply with legal obligations.

Contact
